VR: Left stick: move/strafe | Right stick: turn/fly
Speed: Press thumbstick (L3/R3) for 3x speed
Vertical: Trigger/grip or X/Y buttons for up/down
Teleport: Right trigger (A button) to teleport where pointing
Measure: Y button to toggle | Trigger to place points
Desktop: Drag to orbit | Scroll to zoom | Double-click to focus
